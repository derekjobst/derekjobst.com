Derek Jobst is a software engineer at RaiseMe, a financial technology startup working to improve access to college education. He graduated from the University of Pennsylvania School of Engineering and Applied Sciences in 2017 with a B.S.E. in Computer Science.

Outside of class, Derek was actively involved in the Penn CS and Entrepreneurship community. He was a Student Expert In Residence with the Wharton School, organized the Penn Tech Trek, served as Director of Design and Live Streaming at the PennApps Hackathon, and was the Vice President of University Television (Penn UTV). Derek was a member of the Bell Senior Society, where he served as VP.
